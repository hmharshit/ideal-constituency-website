What is IDEAL Constituency Campaign ?

Ideal Constituency is a Web Based Digital Campaign which Helps The Constituencies To Deal with The Problems and Complaints of
The Ward and Monitize Them For Better Service . It Helps The Constituencies by Providing Them With a Network of Better
Communication Between Public , Government Officials and Councillor of the Constituency . Ideal Constituency Campaign
Provides Constituencies With Better Web Based Products Like The Ideal Constituency Councillors APP , Councillors Web Portal , Which
Help the Councillors to Communicate and Connect With People of the Constituency . There By It Provides a Complaint Portal for
Government Authorities and Public in the Ward Where Public Can Raise the Issues of the Area's . There Complaint's Directly Reach to
The Authorised Government Official and a Copies of The Complaints are Sent to Councillor's Office and Ideal Constituency Team for
Monitisation of the Complaint . Ideal Constituency is an Innitiative for Changing the Political Aspects of Dealing with Problems in the
Lowest Bouned Regions of India Which are reffered as Constituencies . Ideal Constituency Campaign Aims to Bring
Change With The Help of Digital Aspect and Empower The Use of Digital Media at its Best . It is Aiming for a Better Connection and
Connectivity Amongst the Officials , Councillors and Residents of the Area's .

Why There is a Need of IDEAL Constituency Campaign ?

Ideal Constituency Campaign is the Need of Hour , as in Every Region or Constituency the People are Facing Problems in Cordination with the Civic Amenities .
The Lack of Commnication Between the Public , Authorised Officials of Civic Bodies and Councillors has Degraded the Quality of Work Overtime . Most of
The Issues are not Brought up to the Authorities and are Used as Lethal Weapons in Political Campaigning . This Process Results in Loss to Public and Public
Services .To Provide a Better Solution Brandy Solutions and WebSeach InnovationsThe Two Delhi Based Tech Companies Have Taken Innitative Which has Resulted in Ideal
Constituency Campaign Who's Main Motive is to Clear the Dirt of Politics and Make the System Work in a Better and an Organised Way by Implemting Methods of RTI .

Here are Ideal Constituency Campaign's Solutions , And its Features

Daily News Feed

This Portion in the Portal Provides Daily News Feed Whats Happening Around in the Constituency

Event Notification

It Gives Prior Notification For Every Event to be Occuring in Constituency Held By Government

Complaint Portal

This Features Allows Public to Raise There Issues and Register Complaints About There Ward's Problems

Complaint Monitisation

This Department Monitise Complaints of the Public and Provides Connectivity Between Them and Authorities

Government Services

This Developed Portal by Ideal Constituency Helps Public to Reach To Government Services Online in Simple Way

Government Schemes

This Portal by Ideal Constituency Spreads Awareness in the Ward About New Government Schemes & Policies

Government Departments

This Portal Spreads Awareness in the Ward About the Biffurcation of Government Departments and Levels

Government Amenities

This Portal Helps the Public to Find The Government Amentities in the Ward Eg: Ration Shop , Hospitals Etc

Survey Reports

This Survey Portal Help the Councillor , To Reach Out to Public Before Passing an Act , Democraticaly

Search Engine Optimisation

SEO of the Ward By Keywords will Be Done for a Particular ward , So That Finding Amenities Nearby is Easy

Suggestion Implemetation

This Portal is Managed Form Issued Online in Public Interest so That People Can Review & Suggest Authorities

Right To Information

The Right to Information Portal By Ideal Constituency Will Mention All Laws and Rights of People in the Ward
