Welcome To Ideal Constituency Portfolio

Ideal Constituency is an Innitiative for Changing the Political Aspects of Dealing with Problems in the Lowest Bounded Regions of India Which are reffered as Constituencies .It Was a Simple Idea of Monitising Complaints of the Municipal Corporation Departments so as to Give Better Response to the Public in the Ward Which Led to Formation of the Project Ideal Constituency an Innitiative of Brandy Solutions and WebSearch Innovations .

This Idea Innitiated in 2016 and Came into Action Since January 2018 , With First Coucnillor to Adopt this Method and technology for Betterment of his Ward . The Results were Appriciable as 70 % of the Ward Connected with The Portal and 50% of Them Started Adopting the Method to Report to Local Authorities which Gave The Govt. Officials a Releif from Phones , Whatsaap , and Other Methods of Complaints as They were able to Collect Complaints at a Single Place . The Residents Gave a good response as there Complaints were Now Monitised Properly and Quick Resposes were Given . There by Councillors , were Able to Give Themselves a Relief from Authorities , and Dozen of Complaints . The Ward Came into a Systematic Provision there by Connecting Councillor to Residents and Parallely with the Government Officials for Better Work in the Ward .
